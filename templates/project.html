How this project born and was created?

This Twitter Bot was conceived from the challenge of the Zero To Mastery Academy of making a bot, because I was so bored (I'm not gonna lie :D) and with the intention of showcasing my developing skills (and testing them, just a little). I have to specially mention my friends and girlfriend because they were the responsables of giving the Trivia purpose of the bot. Also, they were my main supporters and first testers of the app <3.

From the beggining, my intention was to create different modules with different purposes; translations, Trivia questions/answers and a bot setup, and then mixing them in order to create a complete bot with different features. I started with the translation feature of the bot and for that I created a function which receives a string and returns the translation of into to spanish (to reach a lot of people across the world); thus I used the translate library (available on PyPi). After that, I continued with the development of the Trivia part and it was trickier than I thought (but also I solved it faster than I thought ;) ). I started with the idea of using a little of Web Scraping, with Beautiful Soup, from a page with Trivia questions but I discard that solution after finding a more reliable and long-term solution (the Trivia page doesn't have more than 155 questions) called Trivia API. From that API I was able to recollect a question/answers every time I call the class Trivia created and their methods. Finally, I finished my local job with the implementation and setup of the bot by the creation of distinct functions to give a format to the question/answer and sending the corresponding tweets. But not all were flowers, because I found a lot of user acceptance errors so I needed to change some things to end on my final solution: send the question and reply it with the answer. The post credit part of this story is the upload to Github (which I learned in previous courses and practicing here :D) and the deploy and schedule on Heroku (thanks to them btw).
